Services

Carpentry

Transform your living spaces with our expert carpentry skills. From crafting bespoke cupboards, shelves, and outdoor cooking areas to building stunning decks, floors, and fences, we bring your visions to life with precision and creativity. Our carpentry solutions blend functionality and aesthetics seamlessly.

Electrical work

Illuminate your surroundings with safe and reliable electrical solutions. Our experienced team handles everything from wiring and lighting installation to electrical repairs and troubleshooting. Trust us to power up your space efficiently and ensure your electrical systems run smoothly.

Garden rooms/offices

Escape to your own sanctuary right in your backyard. Our garden rooms and offices are designed to harmonize with your outdoor space while providing a comfortable, versatile environment. Whether you need a tranquil retreat or a productive workspace, we create custom structures tailored to your needs.

Decoration

Elevate your living spaces to new heights with our comprehensive decoration service. Whether you're aiming for a complete house re-decoration or a refreshing refurbishment, our skilled team handles every detail to transform your vision into reality. Our expertise covers both interior and exterior painting and decorating, ensuring that every corner of your home receives the attention it deserves.

General house maintenance and repairs

Maintaining your home's integrity is our priority. Our comprehensive house maintenance and repair services cover everything from fixing leaky faucets and squeaky doors to addressing structural issues. Count on us to keep your home in top condition for comfort and peace of mind.

Kitchen and bathroom installation

Upgrade your essential spaces with our expert kitchen and bathroom installation services. We handle every detail, from plumbing and tiling to cabinetry and fixtures. Experience functional and elegant kitchens and bathrooms that enhance your daily routine and add value to your home.

Plastering

Achieve flawless walls and ceilings with our professional plastering services. Whether you're renovating or starting from scratch, our skilled plasterers ensure a smooth and even surface that serves as the perfect canvas for your interior design ideas.

Plumbing

From fixing leaks and unclogging drains to installing new plumbing systems, our plumbing expertise covers a wide range of needs. Our team ensures that your water systems run efficiently, helping you maintain a comfortable and functional home.

Tiling

Elevate your spaces with exquisite tiling work. Our precision tiling services encompass floors, walls, backsplashes, and more. Whether you're looking for a classic or modern aesthetic, our attention to detail guarantees stunning results that enhance your interiors.

Landscaping

Our tailored designs bring together lush greenery, elegant hardscape features, and sustainable practices to create a harmonious retreat right in your backyard. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, we transform your vision into an outdoor masterpiece that reflects your style and enhances your lifestyle.

About

We are a small family business and pride ourselves on delivering quality and friendly service to all our customers.
We strive to deliver the high standard of work that you expect from skilled tradespeople, nothing less than we would want for our own homes.

You can trust us to give you honest and reasonable estimates for the work(s) you would like completed.
We’ll share over 17 years of experience of house renovations, building decoration and bespoke design and build works with you, as well as bring in other trusted and reliable tradespeople to make your projects and ideas a reality.

Why not get a quote for the work you’ve been meaning to for ages? Contact us now for a free estimate. Competitive rates offered.
